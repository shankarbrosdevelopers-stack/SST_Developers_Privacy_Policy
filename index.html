Permissions

Based on our AndroidManifest.xml, here are the permissions our app currently requires:

1. INTERNET Permission

  • Permission: android.permission.INTERNET
  • Why: Required to load the Google Map in the WebView and to load any network images or data.
  • Type: Normal Permission (Granted automatically at install time).

2. Package Visibility Queries

(Not a permission, but a requirement for Android 11+)

Our app declares that it needs to interact with the following external apps to provide core functionality:

  • Google Maps (com.google.android.apps.maps): To open navigation directly.
  • Web Browsers (https): To open weblinks.
  • Dialer (action.DIAL): To launch the phone dialer.
  • Email Clients (action.SENDTO): To launch email apps.

Important Note

Your app does NOT require sensitive runtime permissions like Location, Camera, or Storage. It relies on external apps (like Google Maps) to handle those features, which is great for user privacy and simplicity.
